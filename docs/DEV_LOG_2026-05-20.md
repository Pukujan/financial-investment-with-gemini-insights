### New metrics per experiment

- **`costEval`** — estimate vs actual tokens and USD (both arms combined), with accuracy rating (`excellent` … `poor`).
- **`efficiency`** — per arm: deviation% per 1k tokens and per $0.01; winner on accuracy-per-token; B vs A gain %.
- **`engineeringInsight`** — OpenRouter summary, recommendations, and concrete prompt tweak bullets.
